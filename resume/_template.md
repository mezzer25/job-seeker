## How to Use This File

Copy this template to `resume.md` (or a role-specific name like `resume-engineering.md`)
and fill it in. The AI reads whichever resume file(s) are present in this folder when
running workflows. See `profile.md` in the repo root for how the two files work together.

- **scan-targets** — compares your background against roles on company career pages to
  estimate fit before you decide whether to create a lead.
- **add-lead** — pre-fills fit notes and surfaces gaps between the job description and
  your experience.
- **job-status-report** — uses your profile and resume together to flag leads where your
  positioning may need work.
